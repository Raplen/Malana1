  <li>Description</li>
  <p>Cloud automation refers to the use of automated processes and tools to manage, provision, and operate cloud computing resources and services. It encompasses a variety of tasks and workflows, from deploying and scaling applications to configuring and monitoring infrastructure, all without manual intervention.</p>
  <li>Features</li>
  <p>Users can request and provision cloud resources such as virtual machines, databases, and storage through a self-service portal or API, reducing the dependency on IT personnel for resource provisioning.</p>
